Crystal Collector!


Rules of the game


  1. Choose any of the crystals below.
  2. Each crystal will give a random number.
  3. If you match your total score to the random number, you win!!
  4. If your total score goes above the random number, you lose. :(
  5. Simply click on each crystal to reveal the hidden number.
  6. New random numbers are revealed with each new game. Good luck! *(^_^)*

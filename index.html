Rules of the game


  • Choose any of the crystals below.

  • Each crystal will give a random number.

  • If you match your total score to the random number, you win!!

  • If your total score goes above the random number, you lose. :(

  • Simply click on each crystal to reveal the hidden number.

  • New random numbers are revealed with each new game.

  • Good luck! *(^_^)*
